CALL FOR PAPERS

Guidelines

  • Original, unpublished papers are invited for presentation in the conference.
  • Selected and peer reviewed articles will be published in the special issue of some known journals.
  • Efforts will also be made to publish the Proceedings of the conference, which will include a selection of the submitted and properly reviewed papers.
  • The papers / abstracts for the conference should preferably be typed in LaTeX format only.
  • The full length paper for the special issue of journals and proceedings should be reached to the convener of the conference not later than January 31, 2018.
  • Participants who would like to present a paper should submit an abstract using Registration tab latest by October 15, 2017.
